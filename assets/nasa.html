The application is made up of five modules which include; Medical Centers, Health Tips, First Aid tips, Speak to a doctor, Help.

1. When the Medical Center icon is selected, an interface that prompts a user to choose a district and medical center of their choice is presented.

-After the user has selected a district and medical center of their choice, they are able to view the location, the shortest route to the center, an approximated cost of transportation to the center and any other information about that center.

2. The Health Tip icon keeps users uptodate with health-care tips, this will help improve the users' lives as these tips are aimed at making sure that the user gets the best health advice.

3. In case a user is interested in finding the best First Aid to give to a patient the First Aid Icon provides tips on how to do just that for the different types of emergencies that may arise before they get to the medical center.

4. Speak to the doctor icon, when pressed this automatically makes a call to the Medical Concierge Group(TMC) who offer free health specialist councelling when the user calls them.

5. The help icon when clicked presents a user with steps on how to use the different icons of the application.
